About MacroSifter

Hi, my name is Fehzan Ali and I am the founder of MacroSifter.

MacroSifter is an investment management software created for individuals interested in enterprise-grade portfolio management. We make it easy for you to connect, track, and analyze your investments across various asset classes.

After spending the last decade investing with advisors, investing in my own ideas, investing in others ideas, trying to time the market, and generally making bad investing decisions, I finally found my firm footing in the world of investing by establishing foundational knowledge in macroeconomics, the way the world works, investment management, and what makes great companies tick. After this time, I was finally able to start generating real investment returns while consistently learning from mistakes.

Most importantly, I learned that investing is overly complex for very few reasons. For everyday investors, the chips are stacked against you with high fees, confusing products, and emotions that can become difficult to navigate during market gyrations.

MacroSifter is meant to replace excel with something better for myself. I believe in this product because it saves me substantial time and allows me to automate tracking and analysis of my various investment portfolios while spotting opportunities, issues, and other key analysis I may otherwise miss. More than that, it helps me stay the course during turbulent times by keeping my eye on the metrics that matter most - the long term fundamentals of my investments.

I hope MacroSifter is that solution for you too.
